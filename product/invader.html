Concept

Designing InVader lamp we combined manual design process with algorithmical one. While plafon was designed manually, the lamp base was developed via interpolation curves. Using the algorithm in Parametric method of design gave us a possibility to develop several variations of the lamp base.

InVader lamp was inspired by the ocean. We tried to represent in it the beauty of its deepness.
